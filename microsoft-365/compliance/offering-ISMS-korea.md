<span data-ttu-id="c05c0-112">K-ISMS 架構是建立於成功資訊安全性策略和原則，以及安全性計數器量值和威脅回應程序，以將任何安全性入侵的影響降至最低。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c05c0-112">The K-ISMS framework is built on successful information security strategies and policies, as well as security counter measures and threat response procedures to minimize the impact of any security breaches.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c05c0-113">這些與 ISO/IEC 27001 控制目標有明顯重疊，但卻不相同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c05c0-113">These have a significant overlap with ISO/IEC 27001 control objectives but are not identical.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c05c0-114">與一般 ISO/IEC 27001 評估相比，K-ISMS 針對要求進行更詳細的調查。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c05c0-114">K-ISMS is more a detailed investigation against requirements than it is a general ISO/IEC 27001 assessment.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c05c0-115">在韓國科學、資通訊與未來規劃部 (MSIT) 監管之下 ([韓文](https://www.msit.go.kr/web/main/main.do)和[英文](https://english.msit.go.kr/english/main/main.do))，韓國網路安全局 (KISA) ([韓文](https://www.kisa.or.kr/main.jsp)和[英文](https://www.kisa.or.kr/eng/main.jsp)) 是 K-ISMS 的認證機構。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c05c0-115">Under the supervision of the Korean Ministry of Science and Information Technology (MSIT) ([Korean](https://www.msit.go.kr/web/main/main.do) and [English](https://english.msit.go.kr/english/main/main.do)), the Korea Internet & Security Agency (KISA) ([Korean](https://www.kisa.or.kr/main.jsp) and [English](https://www.kisa.or.kr/eng/main.jsp)) is the certifying authority of the K-ISMS.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c05c0-116">認證有效期間為三年，而經認證的實體必須通過年度審核來保有認證。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c05c0-116">Certification is valid for three years, and certified entities must pass an annual audit to maintain it.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c05c0-118">根據韓國網路安全局 (KISA) 的嚴格評估，Microsoft Azure 取得韓國資訊安全管理系統 (K-ISMS) 認證，以裝載資料。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c05c0-118">Based on a rigorous evaluation by the Korea Internet & Security Agency (KISA), Microsoft Azure achieved the Korea Information Security Management System (K-ISMS) certification to host data.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c05c0-119">認證涵蓋 Azure 服務，包含計算、儲存體、網路、資料庫和安全性，以及 Microsoft 韓國中部與南部的資料中心基礎結構。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c05c0-119">The certification covers Azure services that encompass compute, storage, networking, databases, and security, and the datacenter infrastructure of the Microsoft Korea Central and Korea South regions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c05c0-120">K-ISMS 認證的規格是根據 ISO/IEC 27001、ISO/IEC 27018 和其他管理資料託管的國際標準。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c05c0-120">The specifications for K-ISMS certification are based on ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 27018, and other international standards that govern the hosting of data.</span></span>
