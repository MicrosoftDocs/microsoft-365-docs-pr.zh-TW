## <a name="mas-and-abs-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="a28f3-105">MAS 和 ABS 概觀</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-105">MAS and ABS Overview</span></span>

### <a name="monetary-authority-of-singapore-mas"></a><span data-ttu-id="a28f3-106">新加坡金融管理局 (MAS)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-106">Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS)</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a28f3-107">2016 年 7 月，新加坡唯一的銀行監管機構及其中央銀行 MAS 發佈了[外包風險管理指導方針](https://www.mas.gov.sg/~/media/MAS/Regulations%20and%20Financial%20Stability/Regulatory%20and%20Supervisory%20Framework/Risk%20Management/Outsourcing%20Guidelines_Jul%202016.pdf)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-107">In July 2016, the MAS, the sole bank regulator in Singapore and its central bank, issued its [Guidelines on Outsourcing Risk Management](https://www.mas.gov.sg/~/media/MAS/Regulations%20and%20Financial%20Stability/Regulatory%20and%20Supervisory%20Framework/Risk%20Management/Outsourcing%20Guidelines_Jul%202016.pdf).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a28f3-108">在指導方針中，MAS 提出了對新加坡金融機構 (包括銀行、保險公司和信託公司) 外包雲端服務的期望。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-108">In the guidelines, the MAS set out its expectations for outsourcing cloud services by financial institutions in Singapore, including banks, insurance companies, and trust companies.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a28f3-109">這是從 2014 年 10 月開始 Microsoft 參與在內整個產業諮詢的結果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-109">This was the result of an industry-wide consultation that began in October 2014 that included Microsoft participation.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a28f3-110">MAS 指導方針能大幅簡化技術採用的流程，明確了解監管機構的期望，並解決了以前減緩金融業採用雲端解決方案的諸多誤解。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-110">The MAS Guidelines substantially streamline the process for technology adoption, provide clarity on the regulator’s expectations, and address many of the misconceptions that had previously slowed the financial industry’s adoption of cloud solutions.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a28f3-111">此外，這些指導方針明確支援金融機構使用雲端服務 (包括公用雲端)，並讓他們從中受益。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-111">Furthermore, the guidelines are unequivocal in their support of the use of cloud services — including a public cloud — by financial institutions and that they stand to benefit from doing so.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a28f3-112">他們消除了金融機構在任何重大外包承諾之前通知 MAS 的期望。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-112">They have eliminated the expectation that financial institutions would notify the MAS before any significant material outsourcing commitments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a28f3-113">相反地，在評估重大外包時，受 MAS 監管的機構應改進其風險型方法，並根據這些指導方針對所有外包安排進行自我評估。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-113">Instead, MAS-regulated institutions are expected to refine their risk-based approach when assessing material outsourcing and conduct a self-assessment of all outsourcing arrangements against these guidelines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a28f3-114">(這些指導方針目前不具有法律效力，但 MAS 表示日後將發佈法定通知。)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-114">(For now, these guidelines are not legally binding, but the MAS has indicated that it will issue a statutory notice in the future.)</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a28f3-116">MAS 外包風險管理指導方針發佈後不久，代表在新加坡營運的本地和外國銀行 (但不包括其他金融機構) 利益的非營利組織 ABS 推出了不具效力的實用指南：[雲端運算實作指南](https://abs.org.sg/docs/library/abs-cloud-computing-implementation-guide.pdf)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-116">Shortly after the release of the MAS Guidelines on Outsourcing Risk Management, the ABS, a non-profit organization representing the interests of local and foreign banks operating in Singapore (but not other financial institutions), introduced a non-binding practical guide, [Cloud Computing Implementation Guide](https://abs.org.sg/docs/library/abs-cloud-computing-implementation-guide.pdf).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a28f3-117">旨在協助銀行按照 MAS 指導方針實作外包安排。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-117">It is designed to help banks implement outsourcing arrangements following MAS Guidelines.</span></span>

## <a name="frequently-asked-questions"></a><span data-ttu-id="a28f3-144">常見問題集</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-144">Frequently asked questions</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a28f3-145">**需要法規批准嗎？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-145">**Is regulatory approval required?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a28f3-146">否，不需要事先通知、諮詢或核准外包安排。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-146">No, there is no requirement for prior notification, consultation, or approval of outsourcing arrangements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a28f3-147">不過，MAS 希望金融機構做好準備，證明他們是如何遵守，並儘快將金融機構的外包安排 (例如資料外洩事件) 所產生的不利發展情況通知 MAS。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-147">However, the MAS expects financial institutions to be ready to demonstrate how they comply, and to notify the MAS as soon as possible of adverse developments arising from a financial institution's outsourcing arrangements — for example, a data breach incident.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a28f3-148">**什麼是「重大」外包安排，為什麼定義很重要？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-148">**What is a “material” outsourcing arrangement and why is the definition important?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a28f3-149">如果服務失敗或外洩有可能對金融公司的業務營運或管理風險和遵守相關法律和法規的能力產生重大影響，則是「重大」外包安排；或涉及客戶資訊，且如果發生未經授權的存取或揭露、遺失或竊取客戶資訊，會對公司的客戶造成重大影響。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-149">An outsourcing arrangement is “material” if a service failure or breach has the potential to materially affect a financial firm’s business operations or ability to manage risk and comply with applicable laws and regulations; or if it involves customer information and, in the event of any unauthorized access or disclosure, loss, or theft of customer information, has a material impact on a firm’s customers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a28f3-150">「客戶資訊」的定義明確排除了安全加密的資訊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-150">The definition of “customer information” expressly excludes securely encrypted information.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a28f3-151">該定義非常重要，因為 MAS 外包指導方針的某些規定僅適用於「重大外包安排」。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-151">This definition is important since certain provisions of MAS Outsourcing Guidelines apply only to “material outsourcing arrangements.”</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a28f3-152">其中包括執行年度檢閱的義務、處理稽核權利的強制性合約條款，並確保在新加坡境外進行的外包不會影響 MAS 的監督工作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a28f3-152">These include an obligation to perform annual reviews, mandatory contractual clauses addressing audit rights, and ensuring that outsourcing outside of Singapore does not affect MAS supervisory efforts.</span></span>
