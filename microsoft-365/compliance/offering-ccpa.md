# <a name="compliance-offering-california-consumer-privacy-act-ccpa"></a><span data-ttu-id="2377b-104">合規性供應項目： 加州消費者隱私權法案 (CCPA)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-104">Compliance offering: California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)</span></span>

## <a name="ccpa-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="2377b-105">CCPA 概觀</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-105">CCPA overview</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2377b-106">加州消費者隱私權法案 (CCPA) 是第一個完整的隱私權法律在美國境內。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-106">The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) is the first comprehensive privacy law in the United States.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2377b-107">它提供各種加州消費者隱私權權限。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-107">It provides a variety of privacy rights to California consumers.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="2377b-108">公司規範的 CCPA 會有數量的義務對那些用戶，包括揭露，一般資料保護規定 (GDPR)-，像是消費者資料主體權利 (Dsr) 的 「 選擇加入延展 」 的特定的資料傳輸及的 「 加入確認程序集 」未成年人獲取需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-108">Businesses regulated by the CCPA will have a number of obligations to those consumers, including disclosures, General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)-like consumer data subject rights (DSRs), an “opt-out” for certain data transfers, and an “opt-in” requirement for minors.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2377b-109">CCPA 僅適用於公司商務加州滿足一或多個項目: （1） 已毛利率的年收益的多個 $25 萬個，或 （2） 超過 50%的其年度收入衍生自加州取用者個人的銷售詳細資訊或 (3) 購買、 銷售或每年共用超過 50000 個加州消費者的個人資訊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-109">The CCPA only applies to companies doing business in California which satisfy one or more of the following: (1) have a gross annual revenue of more than $25 million, or (2) derive more than 50% of their annual income from the sale of California consumer personal information, or (3) buy, sell or share the personal information of more than 50,000 California consumers annually.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2377b-110">CCPA 進入 2020 年 1 月 1，會影響。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-110">The CCPA goes into effect on January 1, 2020.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2377b-111">不過，強制執行由加州律師一般 (AG) 會開始在 2020 年 7 月 1。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-111">However, enforcement by the California Attorney General (AG) will start on July 1, 2020.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2377b-112">加州 AG 會強制執行 CCPA，而必須 power 發出非規範罰鍰等。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-112">The California AG will enforce the CCPA and will have power to issue non-compliance fines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2377b-113">CCPA 也會提供僅限於資料外洩的動作私人右邊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-113">The CCPA also provides a private right of action which is limited to data breaches.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2377b-114">[巨集指令的私用右邊，損害可來自傳來 $100 和 $750 每個用戶的事件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-114">Under the private right of action, damages can come in between $100 and $750 per incident per consumer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2377b-115">加州 AG 也可以強制執行完整 CCPA 佔用不超過 $2500 每違規或蓄意違規每 $7500 民事產生負面影響的能力。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2377b-115">The California AG also can enforce the CCPA in its entirety with the ability to levy a civil penalty of not more than $2,500 per violation or $7,500 per intentional violation.</span></span>
