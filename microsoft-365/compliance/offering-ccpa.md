# <a name="california-consumer-privacy-act-ccpa"></a><span data-ttu-id="06409-104">加州消費者隱私法 (CCPA)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-104">California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)</span></span>

## <a name="ccpa-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="06409-105">CCPA 概觀</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-105">CCPA overview</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-106">加州消費者隱私權法案 (CCPA) 是第一個完整的隱私權法律在美國境內。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-106">The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) is the first comprehensive privacy law in the United States.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="06409-107">它提供各種加州消費者隱私權權限。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-107">It provides a variety of privacy rights to California consumers.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="06409-108">公司規範的 CCPA 會有數量的義務對那些用戶，包括揭露，一般資料保護規定 (GDPR)-，像是消費者資料主體權利 (Dsr) 的 「 選擇加入延展 」 的特定的資料傳輸及的 「 加入確認程序集 」未成年人獲取需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-108">Businesses regulated by the CCPA will have a number of obligations to those consumers, including disclosures, General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)-like consumer data subject rights (DSRs), an “opt-out” for certain data transfers, and an “opt-in” requirement for minors.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-109">CCPA 僅適用於公司商務加州滿足一或多個項目: （1） 已毛利率的年收益的多個 $25 萬個，或 （2） 超過 50%的其年度收入衍生自加州取用者個人的銷售詳細資訊或 (3) 購買、 銷售或每年共用超過 50000 個加州消費者的個人資訊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-109">The CCPA only applies to companies doing business in California which satisfy one or more of the following: (1) have a gross annual revenue of more than $25 million, or (2) derive more than 50% of their annual income from the sale of California consumer personal information, or (3) buy, sell or share the personal information of more than 50,000 California consumers annually.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-110">CCPA 會在 2020 年 1 月 1 日生效。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-110">The CCPA goes into effect on January 1, 2020.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="06409-111">不過，強制執行由加州律師一般 (AG) 會開始在 2020 年 7 月 1。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-111">However, enforcement by the California Attorney General (AG) will start on July 1, 2020.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-112">加州 AG 會強制執行 CCPA，而必須 power 發出非規範罰鍰等。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-112">The California AG will enforce the CCPA and will have power to issue non-compliance fines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="06409-113">CCPA 也會提供僅限於資料外洩的動作私人右邊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-113">The CCPA also provides a private right of action which is limited to data breaches.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="06409-114">根據私人訴訟權，每位消費者每起事件造成的損失在 100 美元到 750 美元之間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-114">Under the private right of action, damages can come in between $100 and $750 per incident per consumer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="06409-115">加州檢察總長也可以全面執行 CCPA，並能夠對每次違規行為處以 2500 美元以下，或對每次故意違規行為處以 7500 美元以下的民事罰鍰。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-115">The California AG also can enforce the CCPA in its entirety with the ability to levy a civil penalty of not more than $2,500 per violation or $7,500 per intentional violation.</span></span>

## <a name="microsoft-and-the-ccpa"></a><span data-ttu-id="06409-116">Microsoft 和 CCPA</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-116">Microsoft and the CCPA</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-117">執行商務加州的商業客戶，Microsoft 將會做為 「 服務提供者 」 相對於我們的線上服務與專業服務提供。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-117">For commercial customers doing business in California, Microsoft will be acting as a “service provider” with respect to our Online Services and Professional Services offering.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="06409-118">線上服務條款 (OST) 和 Microsoft 專業服務資料保護增訂版 (MSDPA) 的字詞已符合 CCPA 下的服務提供者的需求而通常已足夠用來允許要繼續傳輸資料的客戶我們的線上服務。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-118">The terms of the Online Services Terms (OST) and the Microsoft Professional Services Data Protection Addendum (MSDPA) already meet the requirements for Service Providers under the CCPA and are generally sufficient to permit customers to continue to transfer data to our Online Services.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="06409-119">因此，沒有任何其他的契約性變更客戶所要求的能夠自信地仰賴 Microsoft 作為下 CCPA 服務提供者。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-119">As such, no additional contractual changes are required for customers to be able to rely on Microsoft as a Service Provider under the CCPA.</span></span>

## <a name="frequently-asked-questions"></a><span data-ttu-id="06409-135">常見問題集</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-135">Frequently asked questions</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-136">**CCPA 對我的公司有何影響？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-136">**How will the CCPA affect my company?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-137">許多提供 Californians CCPA 的權限都是類似 GDPR 提供，包括洩漏和資料主體權限 (DSR) 要求，例如存取、 刪除及可攜性的權限。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-137">Many of the CCPA’s rights afforded to Californians are similar to the rights the GDPR provides, including the disclosure and data subject right (DSR) requests, such as access, deletion, and portability.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="06409-138">因此，客戶可以查看我們已存在的 GDPR 解決方案，以協助他們具有其 CCPA 合規性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-138">As such, customer can look to our already existing GDPR solutions to help them with their CCPA compliance.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-139">若要開始您應該專注於探索的詳細資訊，判斷如何個人資訊共用、 管理及其使用方式，您 CCPA 旅程如何保護與就地具有正式的資料外洩回應程式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-139">To begin your CCPA journey you should focus on Discovery of information, determining how personal information is shared, governing how it is used, how it is protected and having a formal data breach response program in place.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-140">**GDPR 和 CCPA 有什麼不同？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-140">**What are the differences between GDPR and CCPA?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-141">有許多差異。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-141">There are many differences.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="06409-142">將注意力放在相似點比較容易，包括：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-142">It’s easier to focus on the similarities, including:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="06409-143">透明度/洩漏義務</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-143">Transparency/disclosure obligations,</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="06409-144">消費者權限來存取、 刪除及接收的資料，複本</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-144">Consumer rights to access, delete, and receive a copy of data,</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="06409-145">「 服務提供者 」 的定義是類似於 GDPR 如何定義 「 處理器 」 與類似的契約義務，和</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-145">Definition of “service providers” that is similar to how GDPR defines “processors” with a similar contractual obligation, and</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-148">**公司必須依據 CCPA 支援哪些權利？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-148">**What rights must companies enable under the CCPA?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-149">CCPA 需要管制的企業收集、 傳輸，以及銷售，而在其他方面的個人資訊：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-149">The CCPA requires regulated businesses that collect, transfer, and sell personal information to, among other things:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="06409-151">提供有關來源、 商業用途及類別為收集，包括如何這些類別所售出] 或 [轉接至其他實體的個人資訊的隱私權原則中的更詳細的揭露。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-151">Provide more detailed disclosures in a privacy policy regarding the sources, business purposes, and categories of personal information that is collected, including how those categories are sold or transferred to other entities.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="06409-152">啟用 DSR 權限的存取、 刪除及可攜性已經由您收集的個人資訊的特定部分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-152">Enable DSR rights of access, deletion, and portability for the specific pieces of personal information that has been collected by you.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="06409-153">啟用的控制項，會允許讓取用者退出銷售的消費者的資料。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-153">Enable a control that will permit consumers to opt out of the sale of the consumer’s data.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="06409-154">不過，免除實體，例如服務提供者，將來電轉接會被允許。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-154">However, transfers to exempt entities, such as service providers, will be permitted.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="06409-155">對於下 16，未成年人獲取啟用加入程序，以便沒有銷售次要的個人資訊的可以發生沒有主動選擇集銷售。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-155">For minors, under 16, enable an opt-in process so that no sale of the minor’s personal information can occur without actively opting-in to the sale.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="06409-156">請確保消費者不會因為根據 CCPA 行使任何權力而受到歧視。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-156">Ensure that consumers are not discriminated against for exercising any of their rights under CCPA.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="06409-157">**CCPA 如何適用於兒童？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-157">**How does the CCPA apply to children?**</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="06409-158">CCPA 針對未滿 13 歲的兒童，採用符合兒童線上隱私權保護法 (The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act，COPPA) 的家長同意義務。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="06409-158">CCPA introduces parental consent obligations consistent with The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) for children under the age of 13.</span></span>
