## <a name="what-is-a-legal-hold-notification"></a><span data-ttu-id="59477-106">何謂合法保留通知？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-106">What is a legal hold notification?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="59477-107">法律保留（也稱為「*訴訟暫*止」）通知是從組織法律部門傳送給員工、臨時員工或可能與法律調查相關之資料的保管人的通知。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-107">A legal hold (also known as a *litigation hold*) notice is a notification sent from an organization’s legal department to employees, contingent staff, or custodians of data that may be relevant to a legal investigation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="59477-108">這些通知會指示保管人保留電子化儲存的資訊，以及任何可能與使用中或即將遭到的法律事宜相關的內容。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-108">These notifications instruct custodians to preserve electronically stored information as well as any content that may be relevant to an active or impending legal matter.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="59477-109">法律團隊必須知道每位保管人都已接收、閱讀、瞭解，並同意遵循指定的指示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-109">Legal teams must know that each custodian has received, read, understood, and has agreed to comply with the given instructions.</span></span>

## <a name="the-legal-hold-notification-process"></a><span data-ttu-id="59477-110">法律封存通知程式</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-110">The legal hold notification process</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="59477-111">組織在瞭解即將訴訟或法規調查的情況時，可保留相關的資訊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-111">An organization has a duty to preserve relevant information when it learns about an impending litigation or regulatory investigation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="59477-112">為了符合調查的保留需求，組織應該立即通知潛在的保管人保留相關資訊的責任。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-112">To comply with the preservation requirements of an investigation, the organization should immediately inform potential custodians about their duty to preserve relevant information.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="59477-113">使用高級電子檔探索，法律團隊可建立及自訂其法律封存通知工作流程。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-113">With Advanced eDiscovery, legal teams can create and customize their legal hold notification workflow.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="59477-114">管理員通訊工具可讓法律團隊設定下列通知和工作流程：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-114">The custodian communications tool lets legal teams to configure the following notices and workflows:</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="59477-115">**發行通知：** 從法律部門向保管人發出（或發起）法律封存通知，其可能會有有關案例的相關資訊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-115">**Issuance notice:** A legal hold notice is issued (or initiated) by a notification from the legal department to custodians who may have relevant information about the case matter.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="59477-116">此通告會指示保管人保留任何可能需要的資訊以供探索。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-116">This notice instructs the custodians to preserve any information that may be needed for discovery.</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="59477-117">**重新發佈通知：** 在此情況下，您可能需要保管人以保留之前所要求的內容（或較少的內容）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-117">**Re-Issuance notice:** During a case, custodians may be required to preserve additional content (or less content) than was previously requested.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="59477-118">在此案例中，您可以更新現有的保留通知，並重新將其發佈給保管人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-118">For this scenario, you can update the existing hold notice and re-issue it to custodians.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="59477-119">**發行通知：** 一旦問題得以解決，而且保管人不再受保留需求的制約，就可以從案例發行保管人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-119">**Release notice:** Once a matter is resolved and the custodian is no longer subject to a preservation requirement, the custodian can be released from the case.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="59477-120">此外，您可以通知保管人使用者不再需要保留內容，並提供如何在其資料中繼續其正常工作活動的指示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-120">Additionally, you can notify the custodian that they are no longer required to preserve content, and provide instructions about how to resume their normal work activity with regard to their data.</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="59477-121">**提醒和上報：** 在某些情況下，只發出通知是不夠的，無法滿足法律探索的需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-121">**Reminders and escalations:** In some instances, just issuing a notice isn't enough to satisfy legal discovery requirements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="59477-122">在每個通知中，法律小組可以排程一組提醒和上報工作流程，以自動追蹤未回應的保管人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-122">With each notification, legal teams can schedule a set of reminder and escalation workflows to automatically follow up with unresponsive custodians.</span></span>

   - <span data-ttu-id="59477-123">**提醒：** 在法律保留通知發出或重新簽發給一組保管人後，組織可以設定提醒，以提醒保管人無回應。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-123">**Reminders:** After a legal hold notice has been issued or re-issued to a set of custodians, an organization can set up reminders to alert unresponsive custodians.</span></span>

   - <span data-ttu-id="59477-124">**上報：** 在某些情況下，如果系統管理員在一段時間後仍保持無回應狀態，則法律小組可以設定升級工作流程以通知無回應的保管人及其主管。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="59477-124">**Escalations:** In some cases, if a custodian remains unresponsive even after a set of reminders over a period of time, the legal team can set up an escalation workflow to notify unresponsive custodians and their manager.</span></span>
