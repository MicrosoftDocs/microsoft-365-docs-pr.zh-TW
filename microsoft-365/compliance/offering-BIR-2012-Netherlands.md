## <a name="bir-2012-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="40bd5-105">BIR 2012 概觀</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-105">BIR 2012 overview</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="40bd5-106">在荷蘭政府事業中運作的組織必須示範與基準資訊化人民解放陣線標準 (BIR 2012) 的合規性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-106">Organizations operating in the Netherlands government sector must demonstrate compliance with the Baseline Informatiebeveiliging Rijksdienst standard (BIR 2012).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-107">BIR 2012 提供了以 ISO 27001 和 ISO 27002 為基礎的標準架構。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-107">The BIR 2012 provides a standard framework based on ISO 27001 and ISO 27002.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-108">當使用 Microsoft Azure 或 Office 365 時，這些雲端服務的部分 BIR 2012 控制措施將由 Microsoft 根據雲端計算中的共用責任進行管理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-108">When using Microsoft Azure or Office 365, part of the BIR 2012 controls for these cloud services are managed by Microsoft in line with the shared responsibility model in cloud computing.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-109">因此，需要遵循 BIR 2012 的組織必須決定其使用的基本 Microsoft 服務是否符合 BIR 2012。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-109">Organizations that need to comply with BIR 2012 are therefore required to determine if the underlying Microsoft services they are using are compliant with BIR 2012.</span></span>

## <a name="frequently-asked-questions"></a><span data-ttu-id="40bd5-123">常見問題集</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-123">Frequently asked questions</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="40bd5-124">**Microsoft 是經 BIR 2012 認證？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-124">**Is Microsoft BIR 2012 certified?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="40bd5-125">BIR 合規性的責任適用於政府事業。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-125">The responsibility for BIR compliance is applicable to the government sector.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-126">其要求組織實作資訊安全管理系統，並以適當的技術和組織措施來解決風險。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-126">It requires the organization to implement an information security management system and to address risk with appropriate technical and organizational measures.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-127">對身為雲端服務提供者的 Microsoft 而言，BIR 合規性並非目標，在技術上也不可行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-127">For Microsoft in its role as cloud service provider, BIR compliance is not the objective, nor is it technically feasible.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-128">當客戶實作或使用 Microsoft Online Services 時，這些服務可能處於 BIR 評估範圍內。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-128">When a customer implements or uses Microsoft Online Services, those services may be in scope of a BIR evaluation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-129">不過，組織必須加入屬於整體 BIR 評估一部分的自身 (其他) 控制措施、選擇和流程。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-129">However, the organization must add its own (additional) controls, choices, and processes, which are part of the overall BIR evaluation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-130">此報告的目標是示範政府機關可以透過符合 BIR 2012 規範的方式，採用 Microsoft Online Services。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-130">The objective of the report is to demonstrate that a government agency can adopt the Microsoft Online Services in a manner that is compliant with BIR 2012.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="40bd5-131">**使用 Microsoft Online Services 的客戶是否符合 BIR 2012 規範？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-131">**Is a customer that uses Microsoft Online Services compliant with BIR 2012?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="40bd5-132">示範 BIR 合規性是客戶的責任。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-132">Demonstrating BIR compliance is the responsibility of the customer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-133">使用雲端服務廠商時，客戶通常需要廠商的保證，並加入自己的 (額外) 技術和組織決策、選擇和流程。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-133">When using a cloud services vendor, customers typically demand assurances from the vendor, and add their own (additional) technology and organizational decisions, choices, and processes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-134">這會導致客戶對其 BIR 合規性的整體評估，可以將其提交給第三方稽核者以進行檢閱或認證。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-134">This results in an overall assessment by the customer on its BIR compliance, which can be submitted for review or certification to a third-party auditor.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-135">BIR 涵蓋範圍報告提供 Microsoft Online Services 所涵蓋之 BIR 控制措施的深入解析，但不因此涵蓋端對端的合規性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-135">The BIR coverage report provides insight into what BIR controls are covered by Microsoft Online Services, but as such does not cover end-to-end compliance.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="40bd5-141">**BIR 涵蓋範圍報告是具法律效力的文件嗎？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-141">**Is the BIR coverage report a legal binding document?**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="40bd5-142">否。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-142">No.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-143">這是客戶內部 BIR 保證程序的支援工具，可協助您建立對 BIR 合規性可行的信心和信任。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-143">It is a supporting tool for the customer’s internal BIR assurance process and helps to establish confidence and trust that BIR compliance is feasible.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="40bd5-144">此報告具備描述性狀態，且包含法律免責聲明。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-144">The report has a descriptive status and includes a legal disclaimer.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="40bd5-145">**我們可以分享這份報告嗎？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40bd5-145">**Can we share this report?**</span></span>
