# <a name="financial-industry-regulatory-authority-finra-rule-4511c-united-states"></a><span data-ttu-id="942be-104">金融服務行業規章機關（FINRA） Rule 4511 （c）美國</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-104">Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA) Rule 4511(c) United States</span></span>

## <a name="about-finra-rule-4511"></a><span data-ttu-id="942be-105">關於 FINRA 規則4511</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-105">About FINRA Rule 4511</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="942be-106">[財務行業法規的機關（FINRA）](https://www.finra.org/#/)是調整證券公司的最大獨立身體，在美國監管超過4500的經紀公司。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-106">The [Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA)](https://www.finra.org/#/) is the largest independent body regulating securities firms with oversight of more than 4,500 brokerage firms in the United States.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="942be-107">他是由美國國會所授權，可確保經紀人行業的運作既公平又誠實，以保護美洲的投資者。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-107">It was authorized by the US Congress “to protect America’s investors by making sure that the broker-dealer industry operates fairly and honestly.”</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="942be-108">在2011中，US Security 和 Exchange 傭金（SEC）核准 FINRA 採用 SEC 規則，以管理電子儲存介質上的書和記錄的保留。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-108">In 2011, the US Security and Exchange Commission (SEC) approved the FINRA adoption of SEC rules governing the retention of books and records on electronic storage media.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="942be-109">[FINRA Rule 4511 （c）](https://www.finra.org/sites/default/files/NoticeDocument/p123548.pdf)指定必須依照符合海運（證券 Exchange 法案） Rule 17a-4 的格式和媒體，保留 [所有要在 FINRA 規則中進行的書和記錄。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-109">[FINRA Rule 4511(c)](https://www.finra.org/sites/default/files/NoticeDocument/p123548.pdf) specifies that “all books and records required to be made pursuant to the FINRA rules shall be preserved in a format and media that complies with SEA (Securities Exchange Act) Rule 17a-4.”</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="942be-110">此外，FINRA 規則4511（c）要求公司至少保留六年的帳簿和記錄，而這些書和記錄在適用的 FINRA 或海平面規則下沒有指定的保留期間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-110">Also, FINRA Rule 4511(c) requires firms to preserve for a period of at least six years those books and records for which there is no specified retention period under applicable FINRA or SEA rules.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="942be-111">實際上，如果書籍和記錄屬於某個帳戶，保留期間會規定在帳戶關閉之後六年。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-111">Effectively, if the books and records pertain to an account, the retention period is mandated to be six years following account closure.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="942be-112">否則，保留期間是在這類書和記錄建立之後六年之後。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-112">Otherwise, the retention period is for six years after such books and records are created.</span></span>

## <a name="microsoft-and-finra-rule-4511c"></a><span data-ttu-id="942be-113">Microsoft 和 FINRA 規則4511（c）</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-113">Microsoft and FINRA Rule 4511(c)</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="942be-114">金融服務客戶（如世界上最受管制的行業之一）受到複雜的置備，例如保留金融交易和相關通訊的非擦除和不可修改的狀態。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-114">Financial services customers, representing one of the most heavily regulated industries in the world, are subject to complex provisions like the retention of financial transactions and related communication in a non-erasable and non-modifiable state.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="942be-115">其中一種是金融行業規章機關（FINRA）的規則4511，其可選擇在電子化儲存介質上保留圖書和記錄的管制實體需求 stipulates 嚴格的需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-115">Among them is Rule 4511 of the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA) that stipulates stringent requirements for regulated entities that elect to retain books and records on electronic storage media.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="942be-116">儲存的記錄必須防篡改，但不能變更或刪除，直到指定的保留期間為止。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-116">Records stored must be tamper-proof with no ability to alter or delete them until after the designated retention period.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="942be-117">Microsoft Azure 永恆 Blob 儲存 with Policy Lock 和 Microsoft Office 365 （含保留鎖定）可協助金融機構符合 FINRA 規則4511（c）的不可變的儲存需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="942be-117">Microsoft Azure Immutable Blob Storage with Policy Lock and Microsoft Office 365 with Preservation Lock can help financial institutions meet the immutable storage requirements of FINRA Rule 4511(c).</span></span>
