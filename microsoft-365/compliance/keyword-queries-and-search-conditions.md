### <a name="recipient-expansion"></a><span data-ttu-id="31cc6-231">收件者擴充</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31cc6-231">Recipient expansion</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="31cc6-613">條件會以 **AND** 運算子的邏輯方式連接至關鍵字查詢 (在關鍵字方塊中指定)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31cc6-613">A condition is logically connected to the keyword query (specified in the keyword box) by the **AND** operator.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31cc6-614">這表示結果中包含的項目必須同時滿足關鍵字查詢與條件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31cc6-614">That means that items have to satisfy both the keyword query and the condition to be included in the results.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31cc6-615">這就是條件如何協助您縮小搜尋結果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31cc6-615">This is how conditions help to narrow your results.</span></span>
